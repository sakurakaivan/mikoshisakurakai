Fall Book Sale & Food Fair 2017


October 15, 2017



Event＠Nikkei centerにて、10月14・15日と2日間、日系センターにてイベントが開催されました。

櫻會では、ちらし寿司やお茶漬け・永谷園のふりかけ、Volunteerのまいこちゃん特製手作りバック等を販売しました。
日本では当たり前のちらし寿司やお茶漬けですが

Fall Book Sale + Food Fair + Workshops

October 14 & 15, 2017
10am-4pm both days
Don’t miss out on this chance to grab some fall reads and some great food and workshops!

Book Sale offers a variety of Japanese novels, comics, and some English books, DVD and CDs at bargen prices. Find something interesting for your fall reading at Nikkei Center!

Japanese food vendors will be on site serving delicious Japanese foods and refreshments such as ramen, crepe, and Takoyaki.

Three Workshops (Saturday only) introduce you to Japanese culture.

  • Calligraphy Workshop : 12pm – 2pm : Lecture and Activities
  • Language Exchange : 11am & 2pm: Learn English and Japanese
  • Health Care Workshop (Oriental Medicine): 1pm – 3pm : Lecture and Learn

Proceeds from the sale help support future Nikkei Centre programming and events.
